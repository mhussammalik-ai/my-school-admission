Nova Edge School

Nova Edge School, established in 1980 during the influence of British education, has become one of Pakistan's most respected academic institutions. From humble beginnings, the school grew into a center of excellence, combining global standards with local values. Nova Edge has proudly earned five intermediate-level international awards:

  • Pearson Excellence in Learning Award
  • International Young Achievers Award
  • Global Educators’ Exchange Badge
  • International STEM Innovation Medal
  • Creative Future Schools Award

Recognized across Pakistan for its quality education and student success, Nova Edge School continues to shape the next generation of leaders, innovators, and changemakers.
